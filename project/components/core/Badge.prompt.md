Small mono chip for costs, item types, counts, and status in the GRID STELLA panel.

```jsx
<Badge tone="solid" icon="◈">3</Badge>          {/* gold cost */}
<Badge tone="weapon">WEAPON</Badge>
<Badge tone="buff">BUFF</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" shape="square">×2</Badge>
```

Tones: `gold` (default outline), `solid` (filled gold), `neutral`, and the three item-type tints `weapon | buff | defense`. Shape `pill | square`. Keep labels terse and uppercase.
